How to Play:

1. Click bubbles to pop them and earn points.

2. Larger bubbles are worth more points.

3. Watch out! If too many bubbles fill the screen, you lose!

FAIL CONDITION: Game ends if bubble count exceeds the limit!

4. Choose a difficulty level - harder levels have faster bubble spawns.

5. Try to survive as long as possible and get the highest score!
